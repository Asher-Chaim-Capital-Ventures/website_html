About Us

Asher Chaim Capital Ventures is a boutique multi-family and mixed-use real estate investment & property management company with a track record of building portfolios that deliver dependable cash flow and equity upside. By pooling our capital together, along with a select group of other investors, we can offer nimble capital for real estate equity investments. Our value-add approach allows us to deliver strong returns to our investors. We target value-add multi-family and mixed use properties, and create significant equity by increasing cash flow and forcing appreciation through capital improvements. Our residents and tenants are the lifeblood of our assets. We strive to create vibrant communities where people are excited to live and work.

Our Approach

High Cash Yield

Real Estate stands out from other asset classes as provides the opportunity for direct control of performance unlocking consistently high returns year over year.

Equity Buildup

Monthly payments towards debt via the cash flow of the property increases equity position. The equity multiplier is compounded in value-add strategies that increase the NOI.

Tax Advantage

The tax burden on cash flows and capital gains at disposition of the asset can be mitigated through accelerated depreciation strategies and deferment of tax payment upon investor exit.
